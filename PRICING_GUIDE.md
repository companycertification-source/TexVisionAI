# TexVision AI - Commercial Pricing Guide

## Product Overview

**TexVision AI** is an AI-powered textile and garment quality inspection platform that uses Google Gemini Vision to automate defect detection and quality assessment.

---

## Pricing Plans

### One-Time Setup Fees

| Package | Description | Price |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **Basic Setup** | Installation, configuration, user training (2-3 users) | **$1,500** |
| **Standard Setup** | Basic + custom branding, item master configuration | **$2,000** |
| **Enterprise Setup** | Standard + data migration, API integration, extended training | **$3,000 - $5,000** |

---

### Monthly Subscription Plans

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Features |
|------|---------------|--------------|----------|
| **Starter** | **$199/mo** | $2,149/yr (10% off) | 10,000 images, 2 users, email support (48hr) |
| **Professional** | **$349/mo** | $3,769/yr (10% off) | 30,000 images, 5 users, email + chat (24hr) |
| **Enterprise** | **$599/mo** | $6,469/yr (10% off) | 100,000 images, unlimited users, priority support |

### What's Included in All Plans
- ✓ AI-powered defect detection (Gemini Vision)
- ✓ AQL-based sampling & reporting
- ✓ Item master management
- ✓ Supplier/vendor tracking
- ✓ Inspection history & analytics
- ✓ 1-year data retention
- ✓ Monthly platform updates
- ✓ SSL/secure cloud hosting

## Terms & Conditions (Suggested)

1. **Billing**: Monthly plans billed on the 1st. Annual plans billed upfront.
2. **Cancellation**: 30-day notice for monthly; no refunds for annual.
3. **Usage Overage**: Billed at end of month at stated overage rates.
4. **SLA**: 99.5% uptime guarantee (Enterprise plan).
5. **Data Retention**: 1 year included; extension available at additional cost.
